Prologue

A gentle breeze swept over the meadow.
Walf bear got a warm hug from Nana rabbit.
Simple yet deep words of gratitude was let out.
The sweet grapefruit fragrance oozed out from Walf’s body.
This aroma had saved Nana’s day.

Walf said some words of warm goodbye,
as he felt that his dream was coming to an end.
Nana let go of him, wishing him a good luck.
Walf gave a warm and childish smile,
As his dream started to fade away.

...

A morning light leaks from outside the curtain.
Birds joyful singing has woken Walf up.
This young bear lets out a yawn, and sits on his bed.
“What a wonderful dream that was”. Walf said excitedly,
“I wonder what my next dream will be?”
